The critical problem

Millions of tonnes of paddy straw are routinely burned after harvest, causing seasonal air pollution spikes and large economic & health costs. The single-use plastic ban and low recycling rates increase demand for scalable sustainable alternatives. (Source: PDF — critical problem and stats). :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

  • Air & health impact: Large pollution spikes in Punjab, Haryana, Delhi, and western UP.
  • Raw material abundance: ~23M tonnes of stubble annually — low cost, scalable feedstock.
  • Regulatory push: Plastic bans and ESG mandates create demand for compostable packaging.

Market opportunity

Sustainable packaging is a $70B industry with 12–15% CAGR; stubble-based fiber provides a cheaper, scalable alternative to bioplastics and virgin paperboard. (PDF page 8). :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

Primary targets
Large FMCG (HUL, ITC, Nestlé), e-commerce (Amazon, Flipkart), exports, pharma

Our solution

Agri-Stubble Packaging captures farm stubble → pulps it using clean processing → forms high-performance fiber products that are fully compostable and commercially viable.
